BC Provincial Nominee Program (BC PNP)

The BC PNP allows British Columbia to nominate foreign workers and international graduates for Canadian permanent residence. Nominees must intend to live and work in BC.

Skills Immigration Stream
The Skills Immigration stream is for skilled workers and international graduates with the skills, education and work experience to support BC's economy. Applicants must have a job offer from a BC employer. The minimum wage requirement is $25 per hour for most occupations. Applicants must meet the language requirements of the position.

Express Entry BC Stream
The Express Entry BC stream targets candidates already in the federal Express Entry pool. Candidates must have a valid Express Entry profile. BC will send invitations to candidates who meet BC's criteria. A provincial nomination adds 600 points to the CRS score in the federal Express Entry system.

International Graduate Stream
International graduates from eligible Canadian post-secondary institutions may apply. Applicants must have graduated within the past 3 years. A job offer is required in most cases. Graduates from BC institutions are given priority consideration.

Entrepreneur Immigration
The Entrepreneur Immigration stream is for experienced entrepreneurs who want to establish or purchase a business in BC. Applicants must have a minimum net worth of $600,000 CAD. A minimum investment of $200,000 CAD is required for businesses located outside Metro Vancouver. For businesses in Metro Vancouver, the minimum investment is $400,000 CAD.
